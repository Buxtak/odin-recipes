Gin Tonic Recipe

Description

Gin and tonic made in a highball glass with ice, lime juice, and a lime wedge. So refreshing!

The Allrecipes community adores this lasagna recipe because it's incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list

Ingredients

  • 4 ice cubes.
  • 2 ounces of gin.
  • 4 ounces of tonic water
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 1 lime wedge

How To Prepare

  1. Place ice cubes in a tall, narrow glass.
  2. Pour gin over ice
  3. Add tonic water and lime juice
  4. Stir well with a long-necked spoon
  5. Garnish with lime wedge.
